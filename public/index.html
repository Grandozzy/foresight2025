Songhai Leadership & Business Exchange

Strategic Foresight for Ghanaian Business Leaders

Date: 27 - 28 November 2025

Location: Accra, Ghana

Event Overview

Ghana is rapidly emerging as a regional business hub, offering promising opportunities for entrepreneurs, companies, and investors. To empower business leaders to seize these opportunities and prepare for the future, we are bringing together local leaders with international experts from Belgium for inspiration, knowledge-sharing, and collaboration.

This summit places a central focus on the question: How can businesses in Ghana better anticipate change, position themselves strategically, and collaborate—both locally and internationally—to adapt to demographic growth and build sustainable companies for the future?

In the face of rapid population growth and an evolving economic landscape, our aim is to equip Ghanaian businesses with the tools and insights needed to thrive. This summit will explore how forward-thinking strategies, effective partnerships, and innovative approaches can help businesses not only respond to current market needs but also prepare for tomorrow’s challenges and opportunities.

Key Focus Areas

Through interactive discussions and expert-led sessions, we will address:

  • How can Ghanaian companies respond to demographic changes and shifting market demands?
  • What strategic choices are essential for long-term business success and resilience?
  • How can partnerships—within Ghana and with international players—accelerate innovation and growth?

Together, we will build knowledge, foster connections, and develop new perspectives to help shape a robust and future-ready Ghanaian economy.

Target Audience

This summit is designed for:

  • Entrepreneurs and CEOs
  • Business leaders and managers
  • Investors and business developers
  • Academics and policymakers
