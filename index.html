Why Songhai?

Named after one of Africa's greatest empires, the Songhai Empire, this forum is a call to rediscover our own legacy of trade, innovation, and leadership while connecting the dots globally. The Songhai Exchange is forum for the entrepreneur and the business leaders who believe businesses can transform Africa.

Our Purpose

As Africa enters a critical phase of demographic transformation, businesses are the vehicle into solving the continents problems and need more than tools, they need foresight. This event equips Ghanaian and African entrepreneurs with the knowledge, strategy, and partnerships to thrive in an uncertain world.

This forum places a central focus on the question: How can businesses in Ghana, Africa better anticipate change, position themselves strategically, and collaborate, both locally and internationally, to adapt to demographic growth and build sustainable companies for the future?
